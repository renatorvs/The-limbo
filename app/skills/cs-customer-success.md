# CS — Customer Success Skill

## Workflow
1. Pull CS dashboard: MRR, active customers, NPS, open tickets.
2. Calculate NPS trend and churn risk signals.
3. Identify at-risk customers (health score < 50, open tickets > 3).
4. Recommend retention plays: onboarding fixes, expansion, support escalation.

## Decision Framework
- NPS < 30 → urgent product/support review
- Churn rate > 5%/month → implement health score monitoring
- Open tickets > 10 → prioritize support capacity

## Output Format
- NPS score with promoter/detractor breakdown
- Top 3 retention risks with recommended actions
- Expansion revenue opportunities
